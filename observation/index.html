This is the Dimmable LED Desk Lamp, designed by obscure and elusive electronics company MoKo and released in 2017.

It is the swiss army knife of lamps: in addition to its multiple controls for type of lighting, the lamp has a highly compact/maneuvarable frame and a USB charging port. The many functions and relatively low cost suggest it is targeted for students and others looking for convenient and multipurpose appliances.

Hover over the replicated buttons to learn about their function, and click them to see how they affect the lighting.
